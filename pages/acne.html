Overview

Acne is an inflammatory skin condition related to clogged follicles, excess sebum, bacterial activity, and local inflammation, commonly affecting face, chest, and back.

Common Lesions

  • Blackheads, whiteheads, papules, and pustules
  • Painful nodules or cysts in moderate to severe cases
  • Post-acne marks and risk of scars if delayed treatment

Homeopathic Clinical View

Homeopathic prescribing is individualized by acne pattern, hormonal relation, skin sensitivity, stress, and digestive tendency. It is used as supportive care with proper skin routine.

Moderate to severe acne should be co-managed with dermatology to reduce scarring risk.

When to Seek Medical Treatment

Consult a doctor for painful nodules, frequent flare-ups, persistent breakouts, or signs of scar formation.
