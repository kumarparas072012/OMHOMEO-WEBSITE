Overview

Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS) is a hormonal and metabolic condition that can affect menstrual cycles, ovulation, skin, weight, and fertility.

Common Symptoms

  • Irregular or missed periods
  • Acne, oily skin, or excess facial/body hair
  • Weight gain, insulin resistance, or difficulty conceiving

Homeopathic Clinical View

Homeopathic care is individualized according to cycle pattern, hormonal symptoms, emotional state, metabolism, and skin changes. It is used as supportive care with diet, exercise, sleep regulation, and stress control.

Regular gynecology and endocrinology follow-up remains important.

When to Seek Medical Care

Consult a specialist for prolonged cycle gaps, severe acne/hair changes, infertility concerns, or metabolic risk signs.
