Understanding PCOS

Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS) is a hormonal disorder common among women of reproductive age. It is characterized by a combination of symptoms that can affect a woman's overall health and well-being.

Symptoms

  • Irregular periods
  • Excess hair growth (hirsutism)
  • Weight gain
  • Oily skin or acne
  • Thinning hair or hair loss

Management Strategies

Management of PCOS may include lifestyle changes, medication, and alternative therapies. It is important to consult with a healthcare provider for a personalized treatment plan.

  • Healthy diet and regular exercise
  • Medications to regulate menstrual cycles
  • Hormonal treatments
  • Support for mental health
