Understanding Blemishes

A quick guide to dark spots, post-inflammatory marks, and skin protection.

Home Specializations Contact

Overview

Blemishes include dark spots and uneven pigmentation, often occurring after acne, inflammation, sun exposure, or hormonal changes.

Common Triggers

  • Post-acne or post-rash inflammatory marks
  • Ultraviolet exposure without sun protection
  • Skin irritation, cosmetic sensitivity, or hormonal fluctuation

Homeopathic Clinical View

Homeopathic management is individualized by skin type, pigmentation pattern, and recurrence tendency. It is combined with sun care, gentle skin products, and trigger control.

Persistent or resistant pigmentation may require dermatologist-guided topical or procedural care.

When to Seek Immediate Skin Review

Get prompt medical review if any skin spot rapidly changes color, border, size, or starts bleeding/ulcerating.
