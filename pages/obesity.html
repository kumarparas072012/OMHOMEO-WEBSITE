Overview

Obesity is excess body fat that increases the risk of diabetes, hypertension, heart disease, sleep apnea, and joint problems. It is influenced by metabolic, genetic, behavioral, and environmental factors.

Common Concerns

  • Progressive weight gain and reduced stamina
  • Breathlessness, snoring, body pain, or fatigue
  • Associated blood sugar, thyroid, or lipid abnormalities

Homeopathic Clinical View

Homeopathic support is individualized for appetite pattern, metabolism, emotional eating, sleep, and endocrine profile. Sustainable improvement requires structured diet, activity, and behavioral habits.

Medical screening and regular monitoring are essential for comorbidity prevention.

When to Seek Medical Care

Get medical review for rapid weight gain, severe breathlessness, snoring with daytime sleepiness, or metabolic lab abnormalities.
