Understanding Obesity

Obesity is a complex health issue resulting from a combination of causes and individual factors such as behavior, genetics, metabolism, and environment. It is defined as having an excessive amount of body fat.

Causes of Obesity

  • Genetics
  • Unhealthy diet
  • Lack of physical activity
  • Medical conditions
  • Medications

Health Risks Associated with Obesity

Obesity can lead to various health problems, including:

  • Heart disease
  • Type 2 diabetes
  • High blood pressure
  • Certain cancers
  • Sleep apnea

Management Strategies

Effective management of obesity includes:

  • Healthy eating habits
  • Regular physical activity
  • Behavioral changes
  • Medical interventions
  • Surgery (in severe cases)
