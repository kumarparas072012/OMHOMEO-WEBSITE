General guidance for common hair shedding patterns and integrated care.

Overview

Hair fall can occur due to stress, nutritional deficiency, hormonal imbalance, thyroid disease, illness, medication effects, or genetic tendency.

Common Signs

  • Increased hair shedding while combing or washing
  • Thinning over scalp parting or temples
  • Reduced hair density, rough texture, or slow regrowth

Homeopathic Clinical View

Homeopathic support is individualized according to hair-loss pattern, scalp condition, stress profile, and associated hormonal or nutritional factors.

Best outcomes come with correction of deficiencies, sleep, stress, and scalp care routine.

When to Seek Medical Care

Seek dermatologist review for sudden heavy shedding, scalp inflammation, patchy loss, or hair fall with fatigue, weight changes, or menstrual issues.
