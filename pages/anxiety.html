Overview

Anxiety disorders involve excessive fear, tension, or worry that persists and interferes with work, relationships, sleep, and routine activities.

Common Symptoms

  • Persistent worry, irritability, restlessness, or overthinking
  • Palpitations, sweating, tremors, gut discomfort, or sleep disturbance
  • Difficulty concentrating and avoidance of stress-triggering situations

Homeopathic Clinical View

Homeopathic consultation evaluates emotional triggers, fear profile, sleep quality, and stress response pattern to choose individualized supportive remedies.

Evidence-based care such as counseling and psychiatric treatment should continue as advised.

When to Seek Immediate Help

Seek urgent care for severe panic with breathing difficulty, chest pain, faintness, or self-harm thoughts.
