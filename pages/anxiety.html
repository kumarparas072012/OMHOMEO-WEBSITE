What is Anxiety?

Anxiety disorders are a group of mental health conditions characterized by excessive fear or worry. They can interfere with daily activities and can manifest in various forms, including generalized anxiety disorder, panic disorder, and social anxiety disorder.

Symptoms of Anxiety

  • Restlessness or feeling on edge
  • Fatigue
  • Difficulty concentrating
  • Irritability
  • Muscle tension
  • Sleep disturbances

Management Strategies

Managing anxiety often involves a combination of therapy, medication, and lifestyle changes. Here are some effective strategies:

  • Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)
  • Mindfulness and relaxation techniques
  • Regular physical activity
  • Adequate sleep
  • A balanced diet
