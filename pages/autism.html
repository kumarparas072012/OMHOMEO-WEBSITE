Overview

Autism Spectrum Disorder is a neurodevelopmental condition that can affect social communication, behavior patterns, and sensory processing in different ways for each child.

Common Features

  • Differences in social interaction and communication
  • Repetitive behaviors or highly focused interests
  • Sensory sensitivities and unique learning needs

Homeopathic Clinical View

Homeopathic care can be considered as individualized supportive care for associated concerns such as sleep disturbance, anxiety, irritability, or digestive issues.

Early intervention therapies (speech, occupational, behavioral, educational support) remain the foundation of management.

When to Seek Specialist Review

Seek developmental assessment for delayed speech, social regression, loss of skills, or persistent concerns in communication and behavior.
