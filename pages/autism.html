What is Autism?

Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) is a complex neurodevelopmental disorder characterized by challenges with social interaction, communication, and repetitive behaviors. The symptoms and severity can vary widely among individuals.

Symptoms of Autism

  • Difficulty in understanding social cues
  • Challenges in communication
  • Repetitive behaviors or interests
  • Difficulty in adapting to changes
  • Strong focus on specific topics or activities

Support and Management

While there is no cure for autism, early intervention and tailored support can significantly improve outcomes. Strategies may include:

  • Behavioral therapy
  • Speech and language therapy
  • Occupational therapy
  • Social skills training
  • Medication for associated symptoms
