Overview

Depression is a common mental health condition marked by persistent low mood or loss of interest that affects daily functioning, sleep, appetite, and concentration.

Common Symptoms

  • Persistent sadness, hopelessness, or emotional numbness
  • Low energy, poor sleep, appetite changes, or lack of motivation
  • Withdrawal, guilt, poor concentration, or self-harm thoughts in severe cases

Homeopathic Clinical View

Homeopathic care is selected after detailed case-taking including stress triggers, sleep pattern, emotional expression, and constitutional tendencies. It is supportive, not a replacement for psychiatric care.

Psychological therapy and medical treatment should continue under qualified supervision.

Emergency Warning Signs

If there are suicidal thoughts, plans, or self-harm risk, seek emergency services or a crisis helpline immediately.
