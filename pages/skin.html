Overview

Skin disorders include conditions such as eczema, dermatitis, fungal infections, and inflammatory rashes. Triggers may involve allergy, infection, stress, climate, and immune response.

Common Symptoms

  • Redness, itching, dryness, scaling, or irritation
  • Patchy rash, recurrent eruptions, or local swelling
  • Burning sensation, pigmentation changes, or skin sensitivity

Homeopathic Clinical View

Homeopathic treatment is selected according to lesion pattern, chronicity, constitutional profile, and recurrence triggers. It is combined with gentle skin care, trigger avoidance, and nutrition support.

Suspected infection, severe flare, or non-healing lesions should be assessed medically without delay.

When to Seek Medical Care

Seek timely medical review for painful spreading rash, fever, pus, facial swelling, or sudden severe skin reactions.
