Common Skin Conditions

Skin conditions can vary widely in their symptoms and severity. Here are some common skin issues:

  • Acne: A common condition that occurs when hair follicles become clogged with oil and dead skin cells.
  • Eczema: A condition that makes the skin red and itchy, often chronic and can flare up periodically.
  • Psoriasis: An autoimmune condition that leads to the rapid growth of skin cells, forming scales and red patches.
  • Rosacea: A chronic condition that causes redness and visible blood vessels in the face.
  • Dermatitis: Inflammation of the skin that can result from contact with irritants or allergens.

Treatment Options

Treatment for skin conditions varies based on the specific issue and its severity. Common treatments include:

  • Topical medications (creams, ointments)
  • Oral medications (antibiotics, steroids)
  • Light therapy
  • Moisturizers and emollients
  • Lifestyle changes (diet, stress management)
