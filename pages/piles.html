What are Piles?

Piles, also known as hemorrhoids, are swollen veins in the lower rectum and anus. They can cause discomfort and bleeding.

Symptoms

  • Bleeding during bowel movements
  • Itching or irritation in the anal region
  • Pain or discomfort
  • Swelling around the anus

Causes

Piles can be caused by various factors, including:

  • Straining during bowel movements
  • Chronic constipation or diarrhea
  • Obesity
  • Pregnancy
  • Anal intercourse

Treatment Options

Treatment for piles may include:

  • Over-the-counter creams and ointments
  • Warm baths
  • Dietary changes to include more fiber
  • In severe cases, surgical procedures may be necessary
