What is PCOD?

Polycystic Ovarian Disease (PCOD) is a hormonal disorder common among women of reproductive age. It is characterized by irregular menstrual periods, excess hair growth, acne, and obesity.

Symptoms of PCOD

  • Irregular periods
  • Excessive hair growth (hirsutism)
  • Acne and oily skin
  • Weight gain
  • Thinning hair
  • Darkening of the skin

Treatment Options

Management of PCOD may include lifestyle changes, medications, and in some cases, surgery. Common treatment options include:

  • Weight management through diet and exercise
  • Hormonal contraceptives to regulate menstrual cycles
  • Medications to manage symptoms such as acne and hair growth
  • Fertility treatments for women trying to conceive
