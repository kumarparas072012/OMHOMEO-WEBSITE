Overview

Polycystic Ovarian Disease (PCOD) is associated with hormonal fluctuation and ovulatory dysfunction, often causing cycle irregularity and skin or weight-related changes.

Common Symptoms

  • Irregular periods or delayed cycles
  • Weight gain, acne, or excess hair growth
  • Mood changes, fatigue, and lifestyle-related metabolic stress

Homeopathic Clinical View

Homeopathic prescribing is tailored to menstrual history, constitution, stress response, and associated skin or digestive complaints. Long-term improvement depends on consistent lifestyle correction.

Routine medical monitoring is needed to reduce future fertility and metabolic complications.

When to Seek Medical Care

Seek gynecological advice for persistent irregular periods, abnormal bleeding, fertility concerns, or rapid symptom worsening.
