Overview

Thyroid disorders include hypothyroidism and hyperthyroidism, both of which affect metabolism, energy, mood, menstrual health, and cardiovascular function.

Common Symptoms

  • Fatigue, weight changes, hair fall, or temperature intolerance
  • Palpitations, tremors, anxiety, or sleep disturbances
  • Menstrual irregularity, mood shifts, or neck swelling

Homeopathic Clinical View

Homeopathic care is considered as supportive and individualized according to constitutional symptoms and endocrine pattern. It can complement diet and lifestyle guidance.

Thyroid hormone testing and physician-directed treatment must continue regularly.

When to Seek Urgent Care

Urgent assessment is required for severe palpitations, chest pain, breathlessness, confusion, or sudden neck swelling.
