What is Alopecia?

Alopecia is a medical condition that results in hair loss. It can affect people of all ages and genders.

Types of Alopecia

  • Alopecia Areata
  • Alopecia Totalis
  • Alopecia Universalis
  • Androgenetic Alopecia

Causes of Alopecia

The exact cause of alopecia is not fully understood, but it is believed to be an autoimmune disorder where the immune system attacks hair follicles.

Treatment Options

Treatment for alopecia may include medications, topical treatments, and lifestyle changes. In some cases, hair restoration techniques may be considered.
