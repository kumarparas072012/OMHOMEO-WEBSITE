Overview

Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD) is a neurodevelopmental condition with persistent patterns of inattention, hyperactivity, and impulsivity affecting school, work, or relationships.

Common Features

  • Difficulty sustaining attention and completing tasks
  • Excess activity, restlessness, or impulsive behavior
  • Academic, behavioral, or social functioning challenges

Homeopathic Clinical View

Homeopathic support may be individualized for sleep pattern, irritability, concentration challenges, and emotional regulation in selected cases.

Behavioral therapy, school support, parent guidance, and specialist evaluation remain essential.

When to Seek Specialist Care

Seek pediatric or psychiatric evaluation when attention and behavior issues significantly affect learning, safety, daily routine, or emotional wellbeing.
